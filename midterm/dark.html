why dark mode?

no more eye strain

Dark themes are often preferred in low-light environments, such as during nighttime or in dimly lit rooms.

"I find myself using dark modes more at night when I need to get work done in darker rooms. The glare from light mode is simply too much."

improved visability

Dark backgrounds can make certain types of content, such as images, videos, and graphics, stand out more prominently.

"Contrast plays a big role in creating readability for users. It allows me to more easily distinguish colors against the consistently dark background."

consistency with other applications

Dark themes have become a popular design choice in many applications and platforms. Providing a dark theme option can create a consistent experience for users who prefer this style, aligning with their expectations.

"Most user centered technologies are almost expected to use a sort of light/dark mode as their design base because simplicity is a big marketing point for these technologies."
