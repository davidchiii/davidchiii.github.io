The technology behind cryptocurrency: the blockchain. üîó

The technology behind the blockchain is solid. It relies on cryptographic hash functions that put "blocks" together, each relying on the next's data. This is built-in decentralization, a component that many modern architectures don't have. It's infinitely impossible to alter a blockchain because one party can never have too much control when everyone holds a copy of the same chain.
